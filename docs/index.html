Everything you need

Professional photobooth features without the professional price tag.

Instant Capture

Countdown timer with voice prompt. Photos are captured, processed, and ready in seconds with EXIF rotation handling.

Custom Frame Overlays

Design and upload transparent PNG frames that composite perfectly over every photo. Brand each event uniquely.

Wireless Printing

Print photos directly to any wireless printer connected to your device. No extra hardware needed.

Email & SMS Sharing

Send photos via email with custom templates or SMS gateway. Guests get their photos instantly.

Self-Hosted Upload

Upload to your own Immich server or use anonymous hosting. Your photos, your server, your rules.

Short Video Capture

Optional 8-second MP4 clips with audio. Play back in the gallery, upload, and share via QR — Immich sync included.

Privacy First

Credentials encrypted at rest. No tracking without consent. Offline capable. All photos stored locally.

Up and running in minutes

No servers. No subscriptions. Just install and go.

Install

Download the APK or build from source. Install on any Android 7.0+ device.

Configure

Set your event name, optional frame overlay, and sharing preferences.

Capture

Tap to start. The countdown fires, the camera snaps, and photos are saved instantly.

Share

Guests browse the gallery, get their photo emailed, texted, printed, or uploaded.
